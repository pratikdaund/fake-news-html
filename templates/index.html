Method

      

        The text is lowercased, stripped of URLs and punctuation, and lemmatised.
        Stopwords are removed. The cleaned text is then scored by one of two
        supervised models — either a TF-IDF weighted logistic regression  with a 64-dimensional trainable embedding layer.
      

    


    

      
Limitations

      
    
        
  • Trained on one dataset (ISOT); may not generalise to real-world news.
    • 
        
  • Performs best on English, news-style writing.
    • 
        
  • Cannot detect satire, manipulated imagery, or factual errors in otherwise well-written prose.
    • 
        
  • An academic demonstrator, not a production fact-checker.
    •
