Method

The tool is trained with a TF-IDF preprocessing step and a standard Scikit-learn model. The model learns from frequency-based textual patterns and returns a prediction for the entered article. TF-IDF weighs helpful signals while reducing emphasis on very common words.

Limitations

  • Predictions are dataset-based and may not generalize to real-world cases.
  • Short text can reduce model reliability.
  • Context, source credibility, and nuance may not always be fully captured.
  • A wrong input format can also impact the classifier.
